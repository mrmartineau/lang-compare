C# has a mature ecosystem centered around .NET and NuGet. Strong in enterprise, web, games, and desktop.

**Web Frameworks** - [ASP.NET Core](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/aspnet/core/), [Minimal APIs](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/aspnet/core/fundamentals/minimal-apis), [Blazor (WASM)](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/aspnet/core/blazor/), [Carter](https://github.com/CarterCommunity/Carter)

**Desktop / UI** - [WPF](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/desktop/wpf/), [WinUI 3](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/apps/winui/), [MAUI](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/maui/), [Avalonia](https://avaloniaui.net/), [WinForms](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/desktop/winforms/)

**Game Development** - [Unity](https://unity.com/), [Godot (C# support)](https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/tutorials/scripting/c_sharp/index.html), [MonoGame](https://www.monogame.net/), [Stride](https://www.stride3d.net/)

**ORM / Data** - [Entity Framework Core](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/ef/core/), [Dapper](https://github.com/DapperLib/Dapper), [LINQ to DB](https://github.com/linq2db/linq2db), [NHibernate](https://nhibernate.info/)

**API / Communication** - [gRPC](https://grpc.io/), [SignalR](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/aspnet/core/signalr/), [MassTransit](https://masstransit.io/), [MediatR](https://github.com/jbogard/MediatR), [Refit](https://github.com/reactiveui/refit)

**Serialization** - [System.Text.Json](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/standard/serialization/system-text-json/overview), [Newtonsoft.Json](https://www.newtonsoft.com/json), [MessagePack](https://github.com/MessagePack-CSharp/MessagePack-CSharp)

**Logging** - [Serilog](https://serilog.net/), [NLog](https://nlog-project.org/), [Microsoft.Extensions.Logging](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/core/extensions/logging)

**Testing** - [xUnit](https://xunit.net/), [NUnit](https://nunit.org/), [FluentAssertions](https://fluentassertions.com/), [Moq](https://github.com/devlooped/moq), [NSubstitute](https://nsubstitute.github.io/), [Bogus](https://github.com/bchavez/Bogus)

**Cloud** - [Azure SDK](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/azure/), [AWS SDK](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/sdk-for-net/), [Pulumi](https://www.pulumi.com/)

**CLI** - [System.CommandLine](https://github.com/dotnet/command-line-api), [Spectre.Console](https://spectreconsole.net/), [Cocona](https://github.com/mayuki/Cocona)

**DI / IoC** - [Microsoft.Extensions.DependencyInjection](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/core/extensions/dependency-injection), [Autofac](https://autofac.org/)
