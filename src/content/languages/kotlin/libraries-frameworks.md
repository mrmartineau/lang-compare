Kotlin has access to the entire Java ecosystem plus Kotlin-specific libraries. Strong in Android, server, and multiplatform.

**Android** - [Jetpack Compose](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/compose), [Android KTX](https://developer.android.com/kotlin/ktx), [Hilt (DI)](https://developer.android.com/training/dependency-injection/hilt-android), [Room](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/androidx/releases/room), [Navigation](https://developer.android.com/guide/navigation), [Lifecycle](https://developer.android.com/topic/libraries/architecture/lifecycle)

**Server** - [Ktor](https://ktor.io/), [Spring Boot (Kotlin support)](https://docs.spring.io/spring-boot/reference/features/kotlin.html), [Micronaut](https://micronaut.io/), [http4k](https://www.http4k.org/), [Javalin](https://javalin.io/)

**Multiplatform** - [Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP)](https://www.jetbrains.com/kotlin-multiplatform/), [Compose Multiplatform](https://www.jetbrains.com/compose-multiplatform/), [KMM](https://kmm.icerock.dev/)

**Coroutines** - [kotlinx.coroutines](https://github.com/Kotlin/kotlinx.coroutines), [Flow](https://kotlinlang.org/docs/flow.html), [Channels](https://kotlinlang.org/docs/channels.html)

**Serialization** - [kotlinx.serialization](https://github.com/Kotlin/kotlinx.serialization), [Moshi](https://github.com/square/moshi), [Gson (Java)](https://github.com/google/gson)

**Networking** - [Ktor Client](https://ktor.io/docs/client-create-new-application.html), [OkHttp](https://square.github.io/okhttp/), [Retrofit](https://square.github.io/retrofit/)

**Databases** - [Exposed](https://github.com/JetBrains/Exposed), [SQLDelight](https://github.com/sqldelight/sqldelight), [Room (Android)](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/androidx/releases/room), [ktorm](https://www.ktorm.org/)

**DI** - [Koin](https://insert-koin.io/), [Hilt (Android)](https://developer.android.com/training/dependency-injection/hilt-android), [Kodein](https://kosi-libs.org/kodein/)

**Testing** - [JUnit 5](https://junit.org/junit5/), [Kotest](https://kotest.io/), [MockK](https://mockk.io/), [Turbine (Flow testing)](https://github.com/cashapp/turbine)

**Build** - [Gradle Kotlin DSL](https://docs.gradle.org/current/userguide/kotlin_dsl.html), [Amper (experimental)](https://github.com/JetBrains/amper)

**Desktop** - [Compose for Desktop](https://www.jetbrains.com/help/kotlin-multiplatform-dev/compose-desktop-getting-started.html), [TornadoFX](https://github.com/edvin/tornadofx)

**Functional** - [Arrow (FP library)](https://arrow-kt.io/), [kotlin-result](https://github.com/michaelbull/kotlin-result)
