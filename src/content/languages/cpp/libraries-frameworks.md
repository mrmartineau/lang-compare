C++ has a vast ecosystem spanning systems, games, embedded, and high-performance computing.

**Standard Library** - [STL](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp), [containers](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/container), [algorithms](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/algorithm), [iterators](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/iterator), [ranges](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/ranges), [`<format>`](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/header/format), [`<filesystem>`](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/filesystem)

**GUI / Graphics** - [Qt](https://www.qt.io/), [Dear ImGui](https://github.com/ocornut/imgui), [SDL](https://www.libsdl.org/), [SFML](https://www.sfml-dev.org/), [wxWidgets](https://www.wxwidgets.org/), [GLFW](https://www.glfw.org/)

**Game Engines** - [Unreal Engine](https://www.unrealengine.com/), [Godot](https://godotengine.org/), [Cocos2d-x](https://www.cocos.com/en/cocos2dx)

**Networking** - [Boost.Asio](https://www.boost.org/doc/libs/release/doc/html/boost_asio.html), [cpp-httplib](https://github.com/yhirose/cpp-httplib), [libcurl](https://curl.se/libcurl/), [gRPC](https://grpc.io/)

**Serialization** - [nlohmann/json](https://github.com/nlohmann/json), [protobuf](https://protobuf.dev/), [cereal](https://uscilab.github.io/cereal/), [MessagePack](https://msgpack.org/)

**Databases** - [SQLite (C API)](https://www.sqlite.org/c3ref/intro.html), [SOCI](https://soci.sourceforge.net/), [libpq](https://www.postgresql.org/docs/current/libpq.html), [sqlpp11](https://github.com/rbock/sqlpp11)

**Build Systems** - [CMake](https://cmake.org/), [Meson](https://mesonbuild.com/), [Bazel](https://bazel.build/), [xmake](https://xmake.io/)

**Package Managers** - [vcpkg](https://vcpkg.io/), [Conan](https://conan.io/), [CPM.cmake](https://github.com/cpm-cmake/CPM.cmake)

**Testing** - [Google Test](https://github.com/google/googletest), [Catch2](https://github.com/catchorg/Catch2), [Doctest](https://github.com/doctest/doctest), [Boost.Test](https://www.boost.org/doc/libs/release/libs/test/)

**Concurrency** - [std::thread](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/thread/thread), [std::async](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/thread/async), [Intel TBB](https://github.com/oneapi-src/oneTBB), [OpenMP](https://www.openmp.org/)

**Math / Science** - [Eigen](https://eigen.tuxfamily.org/), [Armadillo](https://arma.sourceforge.net/), [BLAS/LAPACK](https://www.netlib.org/lapack/), [OpenCV](https://opencv.org/)

**Embedded** - [Arduino](https://www.arduino.cc/), [ESP-IDF](https://docs.espressif.com/projects/esp-idf/en/latest/), [Zephyr](https://www.zephyrproject.org/), [mbed](https://os.mbed.com/)
